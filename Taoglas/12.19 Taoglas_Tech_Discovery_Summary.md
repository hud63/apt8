**Tacton Integration Details**
**‚úÖ Current Data Flow**

- Trigger: Deal stage changes to "Quote"
- Data sent to Tacton:
  - Company information
  - Contact information
  - Association labels determine distributor vs end customer

- Data returned from Tacton:
  - Quote preview
  - Product details
  - Note created on deal with attached PDF

**‚ùå Critical Gaps & Pain Points**

- No update sync:
  - "If the salesperson changes on a deal, it's never updated in Tacton"
  - Data only sent once, never resynced

- No pricing/quantity data returned:
  - No costs coming back to HubSpot
  - No prices coming back to HubSpot
  - No quantities coming back to HubSpot
  - "We just have here's what products we've quoted"

- Distributor handling issues:
  - Can only handle one distributor + one end customer per deal
  - Salespeople were putting 4-5 distributors in one deal
  - Creates pipeline reporting problems

- Workflow dependency on "Send Proposal":
  - "We're relying on people to click send proposal, which they don't always do"
  - Incomplete data return to HubSpot

**‚úÖ Desired Improvements**

- Bring back cost, price, and quantity data
- Update proper deal values
- Consider using Quote object instead of Quote Previews
- "We're open to putting it in wherever is the best place to manage it"
