# **Optimization Assessment Playbook**

## **Overview**

Optimization Assessments focus on tenured HubSpot customers who have been using the platform for years but lack a clear strategy for maximizing its potential. These engagements evaluate current HubSpot setup and identify opportunities for improvement, addressing the question "Am I leveraging HubSpot in the best way?"

Ôªø

## **Ideal Customer Profile**

### **Ôªø**

### **Target Characteristics**

* **Organizations with fragmented or inefficient HubSpot usage**  
* **Companies struggling with reporting, automation, or data governance**  
* **Businesses that have evolved but haven't updated their HubSpot processes accordingly**  
* **Tenured HubSpot customers** (typically 2+ years of platform usage)  
* **Multiple HubSpot hubs** with inconsistent configuration or utilization

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Common Pain Points**

* Underutilized HubSpot features and capabilities  
* Inconsistent data quality and reporting accuracy  
* Manual processes that could be automated  
* Disconnected workflows across marketing, sales, and service teams  
* Poor user adoption or training gaps  
* Outdated configurations that don't match current business processes

  ## **Ôªø**

  ## **Pre-Assessment Preparation**

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Current State Documentation Gathering**

* Collect existing HubSpot portal access and administrative credentials  
* Review current subscription level and hub utilization  
* Gather existing documentation (if any) of current processes and workflows  
* Identify key stakeholders across all HubSpot-using departments

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Stakeholder Mapping & Interview Planning**

* Map current HubSpot users by department and role  
* Identify power users, administrators, and casual users  
* Plan interviews with representatives from each user group  
* Schedule system access sessions for hands-on evaluation

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Technical Environment Setup**

* Gain administrative access to client's HubSpot portal  
* Set up analysis framework for portal audit  
* Prepare benchmarking criteria based on industry best practices  
* Configure reporting tools for usage and performance analysis

  ## **Ôªø**

  ## **System Audit Phase**

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Portal Configuration Analysis**

* **Property Architecture Review:** Evaluate custom properties, field usage, and data organization  
* **Object Relationships:** Assess contact, company, deal, and ticket associations  
* **Workflow Assessment:** Analyze existing automation and identify gaps or inefficiencies  
* **Integration Evaluation:** Review third-party connections and data synchronization

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Usage Pattern Analysis**

* **User Adoption Metrics:** Analyze login frequency, feature utilization, and user engagement  
* **Data Quality Assessment:** Evaluate data completeness, accuracy, and consistency  
* **Reporting Utilization:** Review existing reports, dashboards, and analytics usage  
* **Performance Bottlenecks:** Identify slow processes or system limitations

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Feature Utilization Audit**

* **Marketing Hub Analysis:** Email marketing, lead nurturing, social media, SEO tools  
* **Sales Hub Evaluation:** Pipeline management, deal tracking, sales automation  
* **Service Hub Assessment:** Ticketing, knowledge base, customer feedback tools  
* **Operations Hub Review:** Data sync, custom objects, programmable automation

  # **Selling Optimization Assessments Playbook**

  ## **Ôªø**

  ## **Overview**

This methodology provides a systematic approach for identifying, qualifying, and selling Optimization Assessment engagements to existing HubSpot customers. The focus is on companies that have been using HubSpot for years but lack a clear strategy for maximizing its potential, positioning Aptitude 8 as the expert partner to unlock hidden value in their existing investment.

## **Ôªø**

## **Target Customer Identification**

### **Ôªø**

### **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**

* **Tenured HubSpot customers** (2+ years of platform usage)  
* **Multiple HubSpot hubs** with inconsistent configuration or utilization  
* **Growing organizations** where processes haven't scaled with the business  
* **Companies experiencing pain points** despite significant HubSpot investment  
* **Organizations with limited internal HubSpot expertise** or recent team changes

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Customer Segmentation & Prioritization**

  ### **Tier 1 Prospects (Highest Priority)**

* **Enterprise customers** with complex HubSpot implementations  
* **Multi-hub users** with fragmented or inconsistent usage patterns  
* **Recent acquisition targets** needing system consolidation or standardization  
* **Companies with known performance issues** or user adoption challenges

  ### **Tier 2 Prospects (Medium Priority)**

* **Mid-market companies** showing signs of outgrowing current setup  
* **Organizations with new leadership** or recent team changes  
* **Companies expanding into new markets** or business lines  
* **Customers requesting advanced training** or strategic guidance

  ### **Tier 3 Prospects (Lower Priority)**

* **Smaller customers** with basic implementations but growth potential  
* **Recently implemented customers** (6-18 months) showing early optimization needs  
* **Customers with specific feature requests** that indicate broader optimization opportunities

  ## **Ôªø**

  ## **Prospect Qualification Framework**

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Initial Qualification Criteria**

  ### **Business Indicators**

* **HubSpot tenure:** 2+ years of active platform usage  
* **Growth stage:** Expanding business with evolving processes  
* **Team changes:** New hires, role changes, or leadership transitions  
* **Performance gaps:** Declining metrics or plateau in results  
* **Strategic initiatives:** New business objectives requiring platform optimization

  ### **Technical Indicators**

* **Underutilized features:** Advanced capabilities not being leveraged  
* **Data quality issues:** Incomplete, inconsistent, or outdated information  
* **Integration gaps:** Manual processes where automation could apply  
* **Reporting limitations:** Lack of actionable insights or meaningful dashboards  
* **User adoption challenges:** Low engagement or feature utilization

  ### **Organizational Indicators**

* **Resource constraints:** Limited internal expertise or capacity  
* **Change management needs:** Process improvement or standardization requirements  
* **Compliance requirements:** New regulatory or governance needs  
* **Competitive pressure:** Need for operational efficiency or competitive advantage

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Discovery Questions Framework**

  ### **Current State Assessment Questions**

* "How long have you been using HubSpot, and which hubs are you currently leveraging?"  
* "What originally drove your decision to implement HubSpot, and how have your needs evolved?"  
* "Who on your team manages HubSpot day-to-day, and what's their experience level?"  
* "What's working well with your current setup, and where do you see the biggest challenges?"

  ### **Pain Point Identification Questions**

* "What manual processes are your team still doing that you wish could be automated?"  
* "How confident are you in the accuracy and completeness of your HubSpot data?"  
* "Are you getting the insights you need from your current reporting and dashboards?"  
* "How would you rate user adoption across your team, and where do you see gaps?"

  ### **Strategic Alignment Questions**

* "What are your key business objectives for the next 12-18 months?"  
* "How does HubSpot support (or hinder) your ability to achieve those goals?"  
* "If you could wave a magic wand and fix three things about your HubSpot setup, what would they be?"  
* "What would success look like if we could optimize your HubSpot implementation?"

  ## **Value Proposition Development**

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Core Value Propositions**

  ### **Maximize Existing Investment**

* **Message:** "You've already invested significantly in HubSpot‚Äîlet's ensure you're getting maximum return on that investment."  
* **Supporting Points:** Identify underutilized features, eliminate redundant costs, improve efficiency  
* **Proof Points:** ROI improvements from previous optimization engagements

  ### **Expert-Led Assessment**

* **Message:** "Our HubSpot experts will evaluate your setup against industry best practices and identify specific improvement opportunities."  
* **Supporting Points:** Certified expertise, proven methodology, objective assessment  
* **Proof Points:** Team certifications, case studies, industry recognition

  ### **Actionable Improvement Roadmap**

* **Message:** "Receive a prioritized roadmap of specific improvements that will drive measurable business impact."  
* **Supporting Points:** Clear priorities, implementation guidance, measurable outcomes  
* **Proof Points:** Sample roadmaps, success metrics, client testimonials

  ### **Competitive Advantage**

* **Message:** "Optimize your operations to outperform competitors and position for sustainable growth."  
* **Supporting Points:** Operational efficiency, better customer experience, scalable processes  
* **Proof Points:** Performance benchmarks, competitive analysis, growth enablement examples

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Objection Handling Framework**

  ### **"We're doing fine with our current setup"**

* **Response:** "That's great to hear\! Even high-performing teams often find significant opportunities when they take a fresh look at their setup. Our assessment helps identify the difference between 'fine' and 'optimized'‚Äîwould you be interested in understanding what that gap might look like for your team?"  
* **Follow-up:** Share example of similar company that thought they were optimized but found significant improvements

  ### **"We don't have budget for optimization right now"**

* **Response:** "I understand budget is always a consideration. The investment in optimization typically pays for itself quickly through improved efficiency and better results. Would it be helpful to understand what kind of ROI other similar companies have seen from optimization initiatives?"  
* **Follow-up:** Provide ROI examples and discuss potential cost of not optimizing

  ### **"We don't have time for a big project right now"**

* **Response:** "The assessment itself is designed to be minimally disruptive to your team‚Äîwe handle most of the analysis ourselves. The bigger question is whether you can afford not to optimize when small improvements could have significant impact on your results."  
* **Follow-up:** Emphasize low time investment for assessment vs. potential ongoing time savings

  ### **"Our team knows HubSpot pretty well"**

* **Response:** "Your team's HubSpot knowledge is definitely valuable\! Even HubSpot experts benefit from an external perspective and fresh eyes on their setup. We often find optimization opportunities that internal teams miss simply because they're too close to the day-to-day operations."  
* **Follow-up:** Position as complementary to internal expertise, not replacement

# **HubSpot Audit Playbook**

## **Ôªø**

## **Overview**

HubSpot Audits provide comprehensive technical and operational assessments of existing HubSpot portals to identify configuration issues, optimization opportunities, and best practice implementations. These audits serve as foundational analysis for optimization assessments, troubleshooting initiatives, and strategic planning engagements.

## **Ôªø**

## **Audit Scope & Objectives**

### **Ôªø**

### **Primary Audit Goals**

* **Configuration Assessment:** Evaluate current portal setup against HubSpot best practices  
* **Data Quality Analysis:** Assess data integrity, completeness, and organization  
* **Feature Utilization Review:** Identify underused or misconfigured platform capabilities  
* **Performance Optimization:** Discover bottlenecks and efficiency improvement opportunities  
* **Compliance & Security:** Verify adherence to data governance and security standards

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Audit Types**

* **Comprehensive Portal Audit:** Full analysis across all hubs and functionalities  
* **Focused Hub Audit:** Specific analysis of Marketing, Sales, Service, or Operations Hub  
* **Data Quality Audit:** Concentrated assessment of data integrity and organization  
* **Integration Audit:** Evaluation of third-party connections and data synchronization  
* **User Adoption Audit:** Analysis of user engagement and platform utilization

  ## **Ôªø**

  ## **Pre-Audit Preparation**

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Access & Permissions Setup**

* Obtain administrative access to HubSpot portal  
* Verify access to all relevant hubs and tools  
* Ensure reporting and analytics permissions are available  
* Coordinate with client IT team for any access restrictions

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Baseline Data Collection**

* Export current portal configuration documentation  
* Gather user list and permission structure  
* Collect existing process documentation (if available)  
* Identify key stakeholders and subject matter experts

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Audit Framework Setup**

* Define audit scope and specific areas of focus  
* Establish evaluation criteria and scoring methodology  
* Prepare standardized audit checklists and templates  
* Set up analysis tools and reporting frameworks

  ## **Ôªø**

  ## **Technical Configuration Audit**

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Property & Object Architecture**

* **Property Structure:** Evaluate custom properties, naming conventions, and organization  
* **Object Relationships:** Assess contact, company, deal, and ticket associations  
* **Data Types & Validation:** Review field types, required fields, and validation rules  
* **Property Usage:** Analyze property utilization and identify unused or redundant fields

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Workflow & Automation Analysis**

* **Workflow Inventory:** Catalog all active workflows and automation sequences  
* **Logic Assessment:** Evaluate workflow triggers, conditions, and actions  
* **Performance Review:** Analyze workflow execution times and failure rates  
* **Automation Gaps:** Identify manual processes that could be automated

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Integration Configuration Review**

* **Active Integrations:** Inventory all connected third-party applications  
* **Data Synchronization:** Assess bi-directional data flow and mapping accuracy  
* **API Usage:** Review custom integrations and API call efficiency  
* **Error Monitoring:** Identify integration failures and data sync issues

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Reporting & Analytics Setup**

* **Dashboard Configuration:** Evaluate existing dashboards and report structure  
* **Custom Reports:** Assess custom report accuracy and business relevance  
* **Attribution Settings:** Review attribution models and tracking configuration  
* **Analytics Tools:** Evaluate Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and other tracking setups

  ## **Ôªø**

  ## **Data Quality Assessment**

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Data Integrity Analysis**

* **Completeness:** Assess percentage of populated fields across objects  
* **Accuracy:** Identify data inconsistencies and validation errors  
* **Duplication:** Analyze duplicate records and deduplication rules  
* **Standardization:** Review data formatting and naming convention consistency

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Contact & Company Data Review**

* **Contact Segmentation:** Evaluate contact lifecycle stages and lead scoring  
* **Company Properties:** Assess company data completeness and accuracy  
* **Contact-Company Associations:** Review relationship mapping and association rules  
* **Data Sources:** Analyze data entry points and quality by source

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Deal & Revenue Data Analysis**

* **Pipeline Configuration:** Evaluate deal stages, probabilities, and naming conventions  
* **Deal Property Usage:** Assess deal-specific property utilization and accuracy  
* **Revenue Tracking:** Review closed-won amounts and revenue attribution  
* **Forecasting Accuracy:** Analyze historical forecasting vs. actual results

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Service & Ticket Data Evaluation**

* **Ticket Categories:** Review ticket types, priorities, and resolution tracking

* **Knowledge Base:** Evaluate knowledge base content organization and usage

  # **Problem Solving Workshops Playbook**

  ## **Overview**

These sessions help course-correct as new challenges emerge and provide collaborative solutions to complex implementation issues that require stakeholder input and decision-making.

  ## **Workshop Execution Framework**

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Session Structure & Facilitation**

  ### **Problem Definition Phase (15-20 min)**

* Present the specific roadblock or constraint clearly  
* Provide context from previous strategy work (interviews, process maps, POCs)  
* Share supporting data and examples demonstrating the issue  
* Ensure all participants understand the problem scope and business impact

  ### **Collaborative Analysis Phase (20-30 min)**

* Facilitate discussion of root causes and contributing factors  
* Encourage multiple perspectives from different stakeholders  
* Document assumptions and constraints that may be flexible  
* Identify interdependencies with other processes or systems

  ### **Solution Brainstorming Phase (20-30 min)**

* Generate multiple potential approaches and alternatives  
* Evaluate solutions against business requirements and technical constraints  
* Consider short-term workarounds vs. long-term strategic solutions  
* Assess resource requirements and implementation complexity

  ### **Decision & Path Forward Phase (15-20 min)**

* Facilitate stakeholder consensus on preferred approach  
* Document decisions and rationale for future reference  
* Assign ownership and accountability for next steps  
* Establish timeline and success criteria for solution implementation

  ### **Ôªø**

  ### **Workshop Types & Approaches**

  ### **Technical Constraint Workshops**

* Address integration limitations or system capabilities

  ### **Process Optimization Workshops**

* Resolve conflicts between current state processes and future state vision  
* Include process owners and end-users from affected departments  
* Focus on change management and adoption considerations  
* Design transition plans that minimize business disruption

  ### **Requirements Clarification Workshops**

* Address conflicting or unclear requirements from stakeholder interviews  
* Facilitate consensus between different departmental needs  
* Prioritize requirements when resource constraints exist  
* Refine User Stories based on collaborative decisions

  ### **Stakeholder Alignment Workshops**

* Resolve disagreements between departments or leadership  
* Facilitate compromise on competing priorities  
* Align on strategic direction when multiple options exist  
* Build consensus for moving forward with implementation
